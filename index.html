Summary: Latitude vs. X

In this project, the goal was to analyze how temperature changes in different locations when a location approaches the equator. To do this, the OpenWeather API was used to collect data on 500 cities.

To reach the conclusions, python's MatPlotLib and Pandas pacakges were used to analzye data on 500 cities. The visualizations produced looked at the four following factors: cloudiness, humidity, windiness, and temperature. The four visualizations can be explored further on the "Visualizations" tab.
